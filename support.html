How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription through the iOS App Store Subscriptions page:

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone

2. Tap your name at the top

3. Tap Subscriptions

4. Select Momentum: AI Calendar

5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Please note: All purchases are non-refundable. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

To request account deletion, please email us at app.momentum.mobile@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new users. Your subscription will begin automatically at the end of the trial period unless you cancel before then.

How accurate is the AI?

The AI features are designed to assist you with scheduling and calendar management. However, AI-generated suggestions may not always be accurate. Please verify all important appointments, events, and reminders. For more information, see our Terms and Conditions.
